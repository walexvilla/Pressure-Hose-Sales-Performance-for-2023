# Pressure-Hose-Sales-Performance-for-2023
This dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of Pressure Hose sales performance for 2023, covering sales trends, geographic performance, customers, products, and sales representatives. It is designed to help stakeholders quickly identify top performers, trends, and areas of opportunity.

The dashboard tells the story of how pressure hose sales performed throughout 2023, highlighting where revenue was generated, who generated it, what products performed best, and when sales peaked.
Overall, the data shows a business that

Performs strongest in specific cities and regions, especially New York and the East
Relies heavily on high transaction volume rather than very large single transactions
Experiences clear seasonality, with sales peaking toward the end of the year
Is driven by a few high-performing products, customers, and sales representatives
Methodology
The dashboard analysis was conducted using the following approach:

Aggregation of Sales Data
Sales amounts were aggregated by month, city, state, customer, product category, and salesperson to identify patterns and trends.
Comparative Analysis
Performance across cities, regions, products, and sales reps was compared to identify top and bottom performers.
Trend Analysis
Monthly sales trends were analysed to identify seasonality, peaks, and dips in performance.
Distribution Analysis
Transaction values were grouped into ranges to understand whether revenue is driven by many small transactions or fewer large ones.
Filtering and Segmentation
Filters (customer, salesperson, city, region, product) allow deeper drill-down to uncover more specific insights.
